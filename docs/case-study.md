# Case Study: LandBank of the Philippines

## Background

LandBank is the Government Financial Institution designated to serve agriculture and already disburses various government assistance programs.

## Integration Model

**LandBank iAccess / Link.Biz Integration:**
- API connection with x-Change platform
- Real-time fund reservation and settlement
- Automated reconciliation
- Transparency dashboards for auditors

## Expected Outcomes

| Metric | Baseline | Target | Impact |
|--------|----------|--------|--------|
| Cash withdrawal % | 80% | 10% | 70% reduction |
| Reconciliation time | 30 days | Real-time | 100% improvement |
| Leakage rate | 15% | <5% | 67% reduction |

## Benefits

- Enhanced LandBank reputation as "Bank of Integrity"
- Reduced operational risks
- Digital innovation leadership
- Model for other government banks
